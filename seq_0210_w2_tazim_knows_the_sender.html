Foundation Two

The Believer Knows His Lord

Honoring the command honors the Commander

Ibn al-Qayyim unfolds the meaning: the believer comes to know his Lord through the message He sent with His Messenger ﷺ to all people, and what that message demands is submission to His command and prohibition. So a believer's magnifying of the command and the prohibition is itself a proof of his magnifying the One whose command it is. By the measure of that reverence he stands among the الأَبْرَار — the righteous witnessed to have sound faith and freedom from the greater hypocrisy.
