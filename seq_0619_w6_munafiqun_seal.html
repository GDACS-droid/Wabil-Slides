Benefit 68 · The Closing of the Sūra

The Warning at the End of al-Munāfiqūn

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تُلْهِكُمْ أَمْوَالُكُمْ وَلَا أَوْلَادُكُمْ عَن ذِكْرِ اللَّهِ ۚ وَمَن يَفْعَلْ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْخَاسِرُونَ
“O you who believe, let not your wealth or your children distract you from the remembrance of Allah; whoever does so — those are the losers.”
Sūrat al-Munāfiqūn 63:9

Ibn al-Qayyim notes that Allah sealed the chapter of the hypocrites with this verse — for in it is a warning against the very fitna of the hypocrites, who grew heedless of Allah's remembrance and so fell into nifāq. Asked whether the Khawārij were hypocrites, a Companion answered: “No — the hypocrites remember Allah but little.”
