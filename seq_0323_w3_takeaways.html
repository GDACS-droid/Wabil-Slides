Takeaways

What to carry out the door

  • Guard the door of ghafla. The enemy only reaches you off-guard; presence of heart with Allah is the lock on the door.
  • Travel on both wings. Hold together I confess Your favour and I confess my sin — gratitude without conceit, brokenness without despair.
  • Watch the deed after you do it. The danger is rarely the falling; it is standing tall afterward. Guard your acts from riyāʾ and the silent mann.
  • Aim for the middle road. Refuse both the slack license and the anxious, doubting strictness — the enemy will happily take you by either.
  • Polish daily. Make istighfār and dhikr a constant: the cure for the rusting heart, and the bridge into next week.

This week's wird

Anchor each day in Sayyid al-istighfār — morning and evening — letting أَبُوءُ لَكَ بِنِعْمَتِكَ عَلَيَّ وَأَبُوءُ بِذَنْبِي set both wings before the day begins and as it closes.
