Dhikr Is the Head of the Affair

Fawāʾid al-dhikr
37
73

It opens the door of entry upon Allah

Ibn al-Qayyim calls dhikr رَأْسُ الأَمْرِ — the head of the whole affair, the road of the people of Allah, and the patent of nearness to Him. “Whoever is opened to it, the door of entry upon Allah is opened for him. So let him purify himself and enter upon his Lord — he will find with Him everything he wants. For if he finds his Lord, he has found everything; and if he loses his Lord, he has lost everything.”

أَنَّ الذِّكْرَ رَأْسُ الأُمُورِ، وَطَرِيقُ عَامَّةِ الطَّائِفَةِ، وَمَنْشُورُ الوِلَايَةِ
