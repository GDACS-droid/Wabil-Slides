Group breakout
Name the doors the enemy uses on you — and which wing you are missing.
  • Of the three openings — heedlessness, appetite, anger — which one does the enemy use on you most often? Give a concrete example from this week.
  • Of the two wings — witnessing Allah's favour, and seeing your own faults — which comes easily to you, and which do you neglect? What happens to a person who has only one?
  • Have you ever experienced a sin that humbled you toward Allah, or a good deed that made you proud? What was the difference in the heart?
Ten minutes in your group. Pick one honest answer to bring back to the room.
