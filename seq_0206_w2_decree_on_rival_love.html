Foundation One

A Decree That Is Not Turned Back

What Ibn al-Qayyim says Allah has decreed

He lists a chain of consequences for the heart that prefers another over Allah: whoever loves a thing besides Him is punished through it; whoever fears other than Him is given over to the thing he feared; whoever busies himself with something other than Him finds it a misfortune; whoever prefers another to Him is not blessed in it; and whoever pleases another by Allah's displeasure has Allah's displeasure turned upon him.

مَنْ أَحَبَّ شَيْئًا سِوَاهُ عُذِّبَ بِهِ وَلَا بُدّ
“Whoever loves a thing other than Him is, without fail, punished by means of it.”
Ibn al-Qayyim, al-Wābil al-Ṣayyib
