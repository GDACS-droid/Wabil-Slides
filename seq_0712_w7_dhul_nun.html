The Supplication That Was Never Made in Vain

لَّا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
“There is no god but You; glory be to You; truly I have been among the wrongdoers.”
Sūrat al-Anbiyāʾ 21:87

The Prophet ﷺ said of it: “The supplication of my brother Dhū-l-Nūn, when he called upon Allah from inside the whale — no Muslim ever calls upon his Lord by it in anything but that Allah answers him.” (recorded by al-Tirmidhī.) Notice its shape: it opens not with a request but with tahlīl and tasbīḥ — declaring Allah's oneness and transcendence — and only then the servant's confession. The asking is folded inside the praise.
