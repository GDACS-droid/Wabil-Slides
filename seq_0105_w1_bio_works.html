The Author

A Master of Many Sciences

He authored a great many works. A few you will hear named again and again:
  • Madārij al-Sālikīn — his masterwork of tazkiyah, a vast commentary on the stations of the wayfarer to Allah.
  • Zād al-Maʿād — on the guidance (hady) of the Prophet ﷺ in worship, dealings, and daily life.
  • Iʿlām al-Muwaqqiʿīn — a major work of uṣūl al-fiqh and the art of issuing fatwā.
  • Also Miftāḥ Dār al-Saʿāda, al-Dāʾ wa-l-Dawāʾ (the disease and its cure), Ṭarīq al-Hijratayn, and Kitāb al-Rūḥ.
