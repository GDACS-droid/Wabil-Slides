Fawāʾid al-Dhikr

Much Remembrance Is Safety from Hypocrisy

Fawāʾid al-dhikr
68
73

The hypocrites remember Allah but little

Abundant remembrance is a security against nifāq, for Allah described the hypocrites by the scarcity of their dhikr. Kaʿb said: “Whoever remembers Allah much is freed of hypocrisy.” Ibn al-Qayyim adds: Allah is too generous to afflict a remembering heart with hypocrisy — that is the lot of hearts heedless of His remembrance.

أَنَّ كَثْرَةَ ذِكْرِ اللَّهِ أَمَانٌ مِنَ النِّفَاقِ
وَلَا يَذْكُرُونَ اللَّهَ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا
“…and they do not remember Allah except a little.”
Sūrat al-Nisāʾ 4:142
