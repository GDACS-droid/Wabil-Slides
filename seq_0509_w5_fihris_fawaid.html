Fawāʾid al-Dhikr

“In Dhikr There Are About a Hundred Benefits”

وَفِي الذِّكْرِ نَحْوٌ مِنْ مِائَةِ فَائِدَةٍ
“And in the remembrance of Allah there are nearly a hundred benefits.”
Ibn al-Qayyim, al-Wābil al-Ṣayyib (opening of the enumeration)

How we'll proceed

We will not race through all hundred. Tonight we cover the first twenty-five — pausing fully on the ones that revive a dying heart, and clustering the rest. Read each not as a fact to file away, but as a door you are being shown.
