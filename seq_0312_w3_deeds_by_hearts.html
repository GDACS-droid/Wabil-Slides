Two Foundational Rules

It Is the Heart That Weighs

Rule one

  • Deeds outrank one another with Allah by what is in the hearts of faith, sincerity, and love.
  • Two people pray the same prayer; between them in reward is what is between the heavens and the earth.

Rule two

  • A deed wipes away sins in proportion to its completeness — the fuller the deed, the fuller the expiation; the deficient, accordingly.
  • A prayer empty of presence is, he says, like a dead body with no soul in it.

With these two rules many confusions dissolve. This is why the same act of worship cancels much in one person and little in another — the outward form is one, but the hearts are not.
