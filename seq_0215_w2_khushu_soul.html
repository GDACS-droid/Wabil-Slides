A Prayer Without Presence

Among the rights that can be “missed” even while you stand in prayer: khushūʿ, and the presence of the heart before the Lord — الَّذِي هُوَ رُوحُهَا وَلُبُّهَا, which is its very soul and kernel.

A body with no spirit in it

“A prayer with no khushūʿ and no presence,” he says, “is like a dead body with no soul in it” — كَبَدَنٍ مَيِّتٍ لَا رُوحَ فِيه.
