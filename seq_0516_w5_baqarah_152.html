Benefit 15, in the Qurʾān

He Remembers the One Who Remembers Him

فَاذْكُرُونِي أَذْكُرْكُمْ وَاشْكُرُوا لِي وَلَا تَكْفُرُونِ
“So remember Me — I will remember you. And be grateful to Me, and do not deny Me.”
al-Baqarah 2:152
مَنْ ذَكَرَنِي فِي نَفْسِهِ ذَكَرْتُهُ فِي نَفْسِي، وَمَنْ ذَكَرَنِي فِي مَلَإٍ ذَكَرْتُهُ فِي مَلَإٍ خَيْرٍ مِنْهُمْ
Allah says: “Whoever remembers Me within himself, I remember him within Myself; and whoever remembers Me in a gathering, I remember him in a gathering better than them.”
Recorded by al-Bukhārī (#7405) and Muslim (#2675), from Abū Hurayrah
