Benefit 15, in the Qurʾān

He Remembers the One Who Remembers Him

فَاذْكُرُونِي أَذْكُرْكُمْ وَاشْكُرُوا لِي وَلَا تَكْفُرُونِ
“So remember Me — I will remember you. And be grateful to Me, and do not deny Me.”
al-Baqarah 2:152

Benefit 15 — that He remembers you

And in the ḥadīth qudsī (al-Bukhārī #7405, Muslim #2675, from Abū Hurayrah): مَنْ ذَكَرَنِي فِي نَفْسِهِ ذَكَرْتُهُ فِي نَفْسِي، وَمَنْ ذَكَرَنِي فِي مَلَإٍ ذَكَرْتُهُ فِي مَلَإٍ خَيْرٍ مِنْهُمْ — “whoever remembers Me within himself, I remember him within Myself; and whoever remembers Me in a gathering, I remember him in a better gathering than theirs.” The recompense is of the very kind of the act. Ibn al-Qayyim says of this benefit: were there nothing in ذِكْر but this alone, it would be honour and rank enough.
