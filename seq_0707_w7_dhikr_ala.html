The Third Kind · His Favours

Remembering His Blessings and Favours

The third kind is the remembrance of His آلَاء — His blessings, His gifts, His kindness, and the places His bounty has fallen upon His servants. Ibn al-Qayyim calls this too “among the noblest kinds of remembrance.”

To remember a gift is to remember the Giver. This kind is the seed of gratitude: the heart that recalls how it was clothed, fed, guided and forgiven cannot stay heedless of the One who did it. So the three kinds together — His names, His command, His blessings — are, in Ibn al-Qayyim's words, “all of them the servants' remembrance of their Lord.”
