The Greatest of the Fruits

Note the Order: Remembrance First, Then the صَلَاة

Note the order of the verses

Ibn al-Qayyim reads the whole sequence, not one verse. The command to remember much comes first; then, immediately, “He sends His صَلَاة upon you, and His angels.” So this صَلَاة of Allah and of the angels falls on those who remember Him much, and it is the very cause of their being drawn out of the darknesses into light. His conclusion is plain: whomever Allah and His angels send their صَلَاة upon has succeeded with every success and won with every winning — “so what grief for the heedless: what of His good and bounty have they shut themselves out from!”
