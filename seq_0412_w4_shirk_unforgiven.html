The Three Ledgers of Wrong

إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَغْفِرُ أَن يُشْرَكَ بِهِ وَيَغْفِرُ مَا دُونَ ذَٰلِكَ لِمَن يَشَاءُ
“Indeed, Allah does not forgive that a partner be associated with Him, but He forgives what is less than that for whom He wills.”
Sūrat al-Nisāʾ 4:48
  • A ledger Allah does not forgive — shirk; He does not forgive that a partner be set up with Him.
  • A ledger Allah leaves nothing of — the wrongs of people against one another; He exacts every right in full.
  • A ledger Allah may overlook — the slave's wrong against himself, between him and his Lord; the lightest, swiftest to erase, wiped out by tawba, istighfār, the good that effaces, and trials that expiate.
