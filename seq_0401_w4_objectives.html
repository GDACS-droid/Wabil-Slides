Orientation

Where we're going

  • See the slave's lifelong struggle (مُدافَعَة) against three besiegers: al-shayṭān, al-hawā, and al-nafs al-ammāra — and the reinforcements Allah sent against them.
  • Weigh the lowliness of the dunyā beside the ākhira — how little of it is left, and what it costs to sell the next world for it.
  • Learn the long ḥadīth of al-Ḥārith al-Ashʿarī in full: the five words Allah commanded to Yaḥyā b. Zakariyyā and to this umma.
  • Grasp that tawḥīd is the key to the Garden — and the parable of the muwaḥḥid and the mushrik.
  • Sort the three kinds of heart, the parable of the three houses, and how ṣadaqa purifies and ransoms the heart.
