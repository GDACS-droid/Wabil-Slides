Orientation

Where we're going

  • See the slave's lifelong struggle against three commanding masters — al-shayṭān, al-hawā, and al-nafs al-ammāra — and the reinforcements Allah sent: Messenger, Book, angel, al-nafs al-muṭmaʾinna, and light.
  • Weigh the lowliness of the dunyā: how little of it remains, how short the watch (رِبَاط) is, and what it costs to sell the next world for it.
  • Learn the long ḥadīth of al-Ḥārith al-Ashʿarī in full — the five words Allah gave Yaḥyā b. Zakariyyā and this umma — then unpack tawḥīd, the prayer, fasting, charity, and dhikr one by one.
  • Grasp that tawḥīd is the key to the Garden — and that a key with no teeth opens no door.
  • Sort the three kinds of heart, the parable of the three houses, the five ranks in prayer, and how ṣadaqa ransoms the heart and opens the chest.
