Ṣadaʾ al-Qalb

Whom Will You Take as a Guide?

From this āyah Ibn al-Qayyim draws a test for choosing whom to follow. Allah forbade obeying the one who combines three traits: a heart made heedless of His remembrance, the following of desire, and an affair gone to waste.

So look at the one you would take as a guide, a shaykh, an exemplar: is he of the people of dhikr or of the heedless? Is he governed by desire or by revelation? If desire rules him and he is of the heedless, and his affair is in neglect, do not follow him — for he leads only to ruin. But if Allah's remembrance and the following of the Sunna prevail in him, and he is firm in his affair, hold fast to him.
