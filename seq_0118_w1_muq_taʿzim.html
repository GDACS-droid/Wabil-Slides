The Muqaddima

What Makes the Heart Stand Straight

  • First: that the love of Allah outranks every other love in the heart — so that when the love of Allah and the love of anything else collide, the love of Allah prevails, and the heart acts on what that demands.
  • Second: magnifying Allah's command and prohibition (تَعْظِيمُ الأَمْرِ وَالنَّهْي) — which itself flows from magnifying the One who commands and forbids.
مَّا لَكُمْ لَا تَرْجُونَ لِلَّهِ وَقَارًا
“What is the matter with you, that you do not hope for / ascribe to Allah a fitting majesty?”
mā lakum lā tarjūna li-llāhi waqārā
Sūrat Nūḥ 71:13
