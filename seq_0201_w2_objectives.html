Where we're going

  • See that the uprightness of the heart, اسْتِقَامَةُ القَلْب, rests on two foundations, and that the limbs only stay straight when the heart does.
  • Foundation one: putting love of Allah ahead of every other beloved تَقْدِيمُ مَحَبَّةِ اللَّهِ عَلَى كُلِّ مَحْبُوب.
  • Foundation two: magnifying His command and prohibition تَعْظِيمُ الأَمْرِ وَالنَّهْي, and the concrete signs that it is real.
  • Trace these down into the prayer: khushūʿ as the soul of the ṣalāh, and why deeds rise or fall by what is in the heart.
