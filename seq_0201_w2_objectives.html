Orientation

Where we're going

  • See that the uprightness of the heart, اسْتِقَامَةُ القَلْب, rests on two foundations, and that the limbs only go straight when the heart does. So the whole of the religion is being decided in a place no one can see.
  • Foundation one: putting love of Allah ahead of every other beloved تَقْدِيمُ مَحَبَّةِ اللَّهِ عَلَى كُلِّ مَحْبُوب — easy to claim, proven only at the test, and quietly betrayed by the loves we let crowd in front.
  • Foundation two: magnifying His command and prohibition تَعْظِيمُ الأَمْرِ وَالنَّهْي, with the exact signs Ibn al-Qayyim names: guarding the time, the pillars, the perfection — and a heart that actually grieves a missed right.
  • Trace these two down into the prayer: خُشُوع as the soul of the صَلَاة, the “dead gift” offered to a King, and the rule that all deeds rise or fall by what was in the heart, not the bare act.
