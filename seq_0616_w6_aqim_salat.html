Benefit 55

Worship Was Legislated to Establish Remembrance

وَأَقِمِ الصَّلَاةَ لِذِكْرِي
“And establish the prayer for My remembrance.”
Sūrat Ṭā Hā 20:14

Salmān was asked which deed is best; he answered: “Do you not recite the Qurʾān? وَلَذِكْرُ اللَّهِ أَكْبَرُ — and the remembrance of Allah is greater (Q 29:45).” Ibn al-Qayyim cites the Companions that Allah's remembrance of you is greater than your remembrance of Him — and that even the rites of ḥajj, ṭawāf and saʿy were appointed “for the establishing of Allah's remembrance.”
