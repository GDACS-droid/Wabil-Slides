Benefit 42 · The Special Withness

“I Am with My Servant as He Remembers Me”

أَنَا مَعَ عَبْدِي مَا ذَكَرَنِي وَتَحَرَّكَتْ بِي شَفَتَاهُ
“I am with My servant as long as he remembers Me and his lips move with Me.”
Ḥadīth qudsī — suspended (muʿallaq) by al-Bukhārī; graded ṣaḥīḥ by Ibn Ḥibbān & al-Ḥākim, from Abū Hurayra

This is the مَعِيَّة خَاصَّة Ibn al-Qayyim describes — not the general withness of Allah's knowledge that takes in all things, but a nearness of love, protection and aid, as in إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَ الَّذِينَ اتَّقَوْا (Q 16:128) and لَا تَحْزَنْ إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَنَا (Q 9:40). The servant who keeps Allah's company in his ease finds Allah keeping his in hardship.
