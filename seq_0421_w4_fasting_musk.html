Fasting

A Hidden Pouch of Musk

Why did the Prophet ﷺ liken fasting to the man carrying musk? Ibn al-Qayyim draws out the precise point of the image.

Why musk, and why hidden

The pouch is concealed beneath the carrier's clothes, hidden from eyes — and so is the fast: hidden from the sight of people, beyond the reach of their senses, a secret between the slave and his Lord. And the true faster is one whose limbs have fasted from sins, his tongue from lying, obscenity and false speech, his belly from food and drink, his private parts from indecency. If he speaks, he speaks nothing that wounds his fast; if he acts, he does nothing that spoils it — so all that issues from him is wholesome, like the scent that reaches whoever sits beside the bearer of musk. Whoever keeps his company is safe, in his presence, from false speech, lying, wrongdoing.
