Foundation One

What We Put Ahead of Him

Ibn al-Qayyim is blunt about how often we fail this: وَمَا أَكْثَرَ مَا يُقَدِّمُ العَبْدُ مَا يُحِبُّهُ هُوَ وَيَهْوَاهُ.
  • What the servant himself desires — his own hawā.
  • What is loved by someone with power over him — a كَبِير, an أَمِير, a shaykh.
  • What is loved by his own family — placing their pleasure above what Allah loves.
  • In any of these, says Ibn al-Qayyim, love of Allah has not truly come ahead of every beloved, and it is not the love that rules and commands the rest.
