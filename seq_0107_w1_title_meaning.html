The Text

The Abundant Downpour

الوَابِل الصَّيِّب
al-Wābil al-Ṣayyib
the abundant, heavy downpour of rain
الكَلِم الطَّيِّب
al-Kalim al-Ṭayyib
the goodly words — the adhkār that ascend to Allah

What the title pictures

Put the two halves together: the goodly word, when the servant says it, rises up to Allah; and from Allah a heavy rain of mercy and life comes down upon the heart that said it. Remembrance falls on a living heart the way الوَابِل, a downpour, falls on good earth and revives it.
