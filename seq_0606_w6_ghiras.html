Dhikr Builds the Homes of the Garden and Plants Its Trees

Fawāʾid al-dhikr
64
73

The dwellings of Janna are built by dhikr

“The homes of the Garden are built by dhikr; when the rememberer stops, the angels stop building, and when he resumes, they resume.” And as its houses are built by dhikr, so its orchards are planted by it.
