Where we're going tonight

  • Meet the author: Ibn Qayyim al-Jawziyya (d. 751H / 1350, Damascus) — his name, his teachers, and his standing as a master of tazkiyah.
  • Understand the book's title and where it sits among his great works (Madārij al-Sālikīn, Zād al-Maʿād, Iʿlām al-Muwaqqiʿīn).
  • Read the author's own opening: شُكْر · صَبْر · اسْتِغْفَار — and the ʿubūdiyya he begins with.
  • Map the journey of the whole course: the heart's foundations, its diseases, dhikr as its cure, and a daily wird.
  • Settle how we, in our tradition, read a Ḥanbalī master of the heart — faithfully and without quarrel.
