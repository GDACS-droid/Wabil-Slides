Group breakout
Where does love of other-than-Allah actually compete in me?
  • Name one concrete place where your own desire, or pleasing a person with status over you, has won out over what Allah loves. What did the collision feel like?
  • Ibn al-Qayyim says “at the test, a man is honored or disgraced.” What is one small, recurring “test” where you could let the rival love go?
  • Is it possible to love family, a teacher, or your own comfort and still keep love of Allah first? Where is the line between ordered love and rival love?
Ten minutes in your group. Bring back one honest example, not a theory.
