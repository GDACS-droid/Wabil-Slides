Group breakout
Compose, together, a short personal duʿāʾ that follows the ādāb we have just learned: begin with praise of Allah by His attributes, send ṣalāt on the Prophet ﷺ, then ask — laying bare your own need and poverty before the request.
  • Which of Allah's beautiful Names fit the need you are bringing — and how can the opening praise already point toward what you will ask?
  • Where in your draft do you confess your own فَقْر and lowliness, the way Mūsā, Dhū-l-Nūn and Ādam did before asking?
  • Read it back: does it sound like a servant absorbed in his Lord, or like a list of demands? Revise it until the praise carries the request.
Twelve minutes. Each group brings back one composed duʿāʾ to read aloud, and names which ādāb shaped it.
