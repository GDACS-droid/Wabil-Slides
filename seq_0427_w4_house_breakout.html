Group breakout
Which “house” is my heart right now?
  • Empty, king's, or contested — which house best fits your heart this season, and what is the honest evidence?
  • If yours is the contested house, name the “weapon” already inside it that shayṭān keeps reaching for — a particular appetite, doubt, or false hope.
  • What is the “store of faith” you could lay in against that one weapon so the day goes to you, not the enemy — a wird, a companion, a habit removed?
Ten to twelve minutes. This is muḥāsaba, not theory — be specific about your own house.
