Foundation Two

Doing the Deed for People

A man may perform the command and avoid the prohibition and still not be magnifying either. Ibn al-Qayyim names the counterfeit:

Why he obeys

  • For people to see him.
  • To win rank and standing in their eyes طَلَبُ المَنْزِلَةِ وَالجَاه.

Why he abstains

  • So he does not fall in their estimation.
  • Out of fear of worldly penalties — the ḥudūd the Lawgiver set on the prohibitions.

The verdict

Such a man's doing and leaving “does not issue from magnifying the command and the prohibition, nor from magnifying the One who commands and forbids.”
