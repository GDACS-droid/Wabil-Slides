The Muqaddima

Sufficiency Matches Servanthood

أَلَيْسَ اللَّهُ بِكَافٍ عَبْدَهُ
“Is Allah not sufficient for His servant?”
a-laysa llāhu bi-kāfin ʿabdah
Sūrat al-Zumar 39:36

His rule

Whoever is a servant to Allah in both states — upholding His right in what he hates and in what he loves — is the one this āyah embraces. Ibn al-Qayyim draws the measure out: الكِفَايَةُ التَّامَّةُ مَعَ العُبُودِيَّةِ التَّامَّةِ، وَالنَّاقِصَةُ مَعَ النَّاقِصَةِ — complete sufficiency comes with complete servanthood, and the deficient with the deficient.
