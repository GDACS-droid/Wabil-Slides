Istiqāmat al-Qalb

Two Pillars of an Upright Heart

  • That the love of Allah outranks every other love. When love of Allah and love of anything else collide, the love of Allah wins — and you act on what that requires. “How easy this is in word,” he says, “and how hard in deed; at the test a man is honoured or disgraced.”
  • That you magnify His command and prohibition. This grows out of magnifying the Commander Himself. Allah reproached those who do not honour Him with the awe He deserves.
مَّا لَكُمْ لَا تَرْجُونَ لِلَّهِ وَقَارًا
“What is the matter with you, that you do not hope in Allah for dignity?” — explained by the salaf as: that you do not fear the greatness of Allah.
Sūrat Nūḥ 71:13
