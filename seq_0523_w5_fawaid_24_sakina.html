Fawāʾid al-Dhikr

It Brings Down Tranquillity and Mercy

لَا يَقْعُدُ قَوْمٌ يَذْكُرُونَ اللَّهَ إِلَّا حَفَّتْهُمُ الْمَلَائِكَةُ، وَغَشِيَتْهُمُ الرَّحْمَةُ، وَنَزَلَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ السَّكِينَةُ، وَذَكَرَهُمُ اللَّهُ فِيمَنْ عِنْدَهُ
“No people sit remembering Allah but that the angels encircle them, mercy covers them, tranquillity descends upon them, and Allah mentions them among those who are with Him.”
Recorded by Muslim (#2700), from Abū Hurayrah and Abū Saʿīd

Benefit 24 — and 25, briefly

Here is the proof of the twenty-fourth benefit: dhikr is a cause for the descent of sakīna, the covering of mercy, and the encircling of the angels. And the twenty-fifth: dhikr keeps the tongue busy away from backbiting, slander, lies, and falsehood — for the tongue will speak; if not with His name, then with sin.
