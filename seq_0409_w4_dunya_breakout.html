Group breakout
What is the دُنْيَا costing my آخِرَة?
  • Name one thing you are pursuing now where you have, in effect, “begun with your share of the dunyā.” What did it cost the ākhira?
  • “Only as much remains as remains of this day” — and on the Day it will feel like “part of a day.” If that is literally true of your own life, what changes about today?
  • Where in your week do you sell the ākhira for the dunyā — and where could the same act, reordered, win both?
Ten to twelve minutes. Bring back one concrete “reorder” each person will try this week.
