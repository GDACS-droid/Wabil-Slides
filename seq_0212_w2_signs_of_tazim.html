ʿAlāmāt al-Taʿẓīm

What Reverence Looks Like

  • Guarding their times and limits رِعَايَةُ أَوْقَاتِهَا وَحُدُودِهَا — keeping the appointment, not letting it slide.
  • Inspecting their pillars, obligations, and completions — checking the arkān and wājibāt, not just scraping by.
  • Eagerness to perfect them and to do them in their times, hurrying to them the moment they fall due المُسَارَعَةُ إِلَيْهَا عِنْدَ وُجُوبِهَا.
  • Grief and sorrow when any of their rights is missed الحُزْنُ وَالكَآبَةُ وَالأَسَفُ عِنْدَ فَوَاتِ حَقٍّ مِنْ حُقُوقِهَا — the heart actually mourns the loss.
