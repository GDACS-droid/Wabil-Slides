Faṣl 75 · The Comprehensive Duʿās

The Best Thing to Ask: Pardon and Well-Being

تَسْأَلُ اللَّهَ العَفْوَ وَالعَافِيَةَ، فَإِذَا أُعْطِيتَ ذَلِكَ فَقَدْ أَفْلَحْتَ
“Ask Allah for pardon and well-being (al-ʿafw wa-l-ʿāfiya); for if you are given that, you have succeeded.”
From the ḥadīth of Anas — recorded by al-Tirmidhī

Ibn al-Qayyim closes the duʿāʾ chapters with the comprehensive supplications. ʿĀʾisha was told: if you reach Laylat al-Qadr, say “اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ العَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي” (al-Tirmidhī, ṣaḥīḥ). And the Prophet ﷺ taught: “No one is given, after certainty (al-yaqīn), anything better than well-being.” This is where reliance, asking, and the return all rest — a heart at peace with Allah, asking Him for pardon and ʿāfiya.
