It Clothes the Face in Freshness and Light

Fawāʾid al-dhikr
70
73

Freshness in this world, light in the next

Dhikr clothes the face in نَضْرَة in this world and نُور in the next: the rememberers are the most radiant of people in face here, and the most luminous hereafter. This closes the thread Ibn al-Qayyim began earlier — that dhikr gives light to the heart, the face and the limbs, and is the servant's light in his world, his barzakh, and on the Day of Rising.

أَنَّهُ يَكْسُو الوَجْهَ نَضْرَةً فِي الدُّنْيَا، وَنُورًا فِي الآخِرَةِ
