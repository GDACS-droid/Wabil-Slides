The Hook

The Limbs Follow the Heart

Ibn al-Qayyim says the servant's path stays straight only by the uprightness of his heart and his limbs, and the limbs take their orders from the heart.

Two pillars under one roof

He then narrows it: فَاسْتِقَامَةُ القَلْبِ بِشَيْئَيْن — the heart itself becomes upright through two things. Everything this week hangs on these two. The first is a matter of love; the second is a matter of reverence.
