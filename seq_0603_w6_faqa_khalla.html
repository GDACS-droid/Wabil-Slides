A Want Nothing Else Can Close

Fawāʾid al-dhikr
38
73

In the heart is a poverty only dhikr can close

There is in the heart a خَلَّة — a gap, a need — that nothing at all fills except the remembrance of Allah. When dhikr becomes the heart's own habit, so that the heart is the rememberer and the tongue merely follows it, that is the dhikr that closes the gap and answers the want. Then its owner is غَنِيًّا بِلَا مَال، عَزِيزًا بِلَا عَشِيرَة، مَهِيبًا بِلَا سُلْطَان — rich without wealth, mighty without a clan, awed without authority. And the one heedless of Allah's remembrance is the reverse: poor amid his riches, lowly amid his power, contemptible amid his many kin.
