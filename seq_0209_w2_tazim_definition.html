Foundation Two

Three Ways It Goes Wrong

Ibn al-Qayyim quotes his teacher's fine definition of magnifying the command and the prohibition — that they not be met:
  • with a slack license بِتَرْخِيصٍ جَافٍّ — explaining the order away to make it easy on yourself.
  • with excessive severity بِتَشْدِيدٍ غَالٍ — piling on harshness beyond what Allah asked.
  • nor be hung on a pretext that weakens obedience عَلَى عِلَّةٍ تُوهِنُ الانْقِيَاد — finding a rationale that loosens your submission.
  • True reverence keeps to the order as it was given, without softening it and without reaching for an excuse to obey less.
