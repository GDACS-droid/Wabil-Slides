It is the nourishment (qūt) of the heart and the spirit

قُوتُ الْقَلْبِ وَالرُّوحِ. If Benefit 16 is water, this is bread: deprive the heart of dhikr, Ibn al-Qayyim says, and it is like a body kept from its food. Then he gives the living proof. He once saw Ibn Taymiyya pray Fajr and sit remembering Allah until nearly midday; turning to him he said: “This is my morning meal — were I not to take this breakfast, my strength would fall.” And once: “I never leave the dhikr except intending to give my soul rest, so that by that rest I may ready myself for another remembrance.”

هَذِهِ غَدْوَتِي، وَلَوْ لَمْ أَتَغَدَّ هَذَا الْغَدَاءَ لَسَقَطَتْ قُوَّتِي
