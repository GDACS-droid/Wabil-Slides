Group breakout
Name the door the enemy uses on you — and which wing you are missing.
  • Of the three openings — heedlessness, appetite, anger — which does the enemy use on you most often? Name a concrete moment from this past week when the door stood open.
  • Of the two wings — witnessing Allah's favour, and seeing your own faults — which comes easily, and which do you neglect? What does a person who flies on only one wing actually look like?
  • Ibn al-Qayyim says no veil is thicker than a claim. Where do you catch yourself feeling you have something to show Allah — and what would it mean to enter instead by the door of bankruptcy?
Ten to twelve minutes in your group. Bring back one honest answer to the room.
