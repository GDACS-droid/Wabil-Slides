The Journey

Where the Course Goes

  • The foundations: the heart that loves Allah above all and magnifies His command — the upright heart we met tonight.
  • The diseases: heedlessness, the three doors the enemy enters by (forgetfulness, desire, anger), and the dead and the sick heart.
  • The cure: dhikr — and Ibn al-Qayyim's long count of its fawāʾid, the benefits it brings to the heart and the limbs.
  • The wird: the prophetic adhkār themselves, arranged as a daily practice we close the course by taking on.

How to study with us

Read the assigned matn before each session, sit with the Arabic of the proofs, and — most of all — act on one thing each week. This is a book that was written to be lived, not only learned.
