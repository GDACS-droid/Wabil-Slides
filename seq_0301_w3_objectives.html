Orientation

Where we're going

  • Learn how the Shayṭān reaches the believer — he comes only by ambush, through the door of الغَفْلَة (heedlessness).
  • Trace what sin does to the heart: how it can break and humble it, and how that very brokenness can become a door to Allah.
  • Distinguish tawfīq (Allah not leaving you to yourself) from khidhlān (His leaving you to yourself).
  • Study the nullifiers of deeds — riyāʾ and its kin — and the hard case of the repentant show-off.
  • Walk the straight path between two pits: lax license and excess strictness with obsessive doubt.
  • End where the cure begins: the heart rusts by two things and is polished by two — this sets up dhikr.
