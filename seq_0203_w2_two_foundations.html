The Architecture

Love First, Reverence Throughout

First foundation

  • أَنْ تَكُونَ مَحَبَّةُ اللَّهِ تَتَقَدَّمُ عِنْدَهُ عَلَى جَمِيعِ المَحَابّ
  • That love of Allah comes ahead of every beloved.
  • So that when love of Allah and love of anything else collide, love of Allah wins, and you act on what that love requires.

Second foundation

  • تَعْظِيمُ الأَمْرِ وَالنَّهْي
  • Magnifying His command and His prohibition.
  • Which grows out of magnifying the One who commands and forbids: you revere the order because you revere the Sender.
