The Text

The Abundant Downpour

الوَابِل الصَّيِّب
the abundant, heavy, soaking downpour of rain
الكَلِم الطَّيِّب
the goodly words — the أَذْكَار that ascend to Allah

What the title pictures

Put the two halves together and the whole book is in the image: the goodly word, when the servant says it, rises to Allah; and from Allah a heavy rain of mercy and life comes down upon the heart that said it. Remembrance falls on a living heart the way الوَابِل, a soaking downpour, falls on good earth — and what was dry and cracked turns green. The book's full name keeps both directions in view: al-Wābil al-Ṣayyib min al-Kalim al-Ṭayyib — “the abundant downpour, from the goodly word.”
